Get to know the Permian extinction.

The Permian Extinction was the most severe mass extinction event in Earth's history. It occurred approximately 252 million years ago, marking the end of the Permian period and the beginning of the Triassic period. The extinction event wiped out over 90% of marine and 70% of terrestrial species, leading to a dramatic reshaping of the Earth's ecosystems.
